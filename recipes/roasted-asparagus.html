Roasted Asparagus Recipe

This is a simple roasted asparagus recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of asparagus
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Trim the ends of the asparagus and toss with oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast for 10-12 minutes until tender and slightly browned.
