Fried Potatoes Recipe

This is a simple fried potatoes recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of potatoes
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut into fry-shaped pieces.
  2. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Fry the potatoes for 5-7 minutes until golden brown.
